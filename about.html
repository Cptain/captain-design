    My name is Nicholas, my friends call me Niko or Captain, hence 'Captain Design'! I'm a Toronto based front-end developer and graphic designer with a passion for UI design.

    I love CSS and everything that has an impact on how we view the web. When I was a teenager, I decided to teach myself HTML and CSS for fun. Three years ago, during my graphic design program, I took the opportunity to turn it in a profession. Now, as a graphic design graduate I have a deeper understanding of layout, typography, branding, and animation, that further enhance my web design skills.

    On my free time I'm almost always hanging out with my wonderful wife, or I'm enjoying whatever video game I'm currently into. When the weather allows it or if I'm feeling brave (during winter), I also enjoy the outdoors a lot.
